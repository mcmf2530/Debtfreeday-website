Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 20, 2026

Debt Free Day ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when you create an account or use the app:

  • Name and email address (for account creation)
  • Debt information you enter (balances, interest rates, minimum payments)
  • Payment records you log within the app
  • Device information and anonymized usage analytics

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve the Debt Free Day app
  • Calculate your personalized debt payoff projections
  • Enable partner sharing features when you choose to use them
  • Send account-related notifications
  • Respond to your support requests

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase with industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Financial data is used solely to power your in-app calculations and is never shared with financial institutions or advertisers.

Partner Sharing

If you invite a partner, they can view your debt totals, Debt Free Day date, and payment confirmations. Payment notes and history remain private. You can revoke partner access at any time from Settings.

Data Deletion

You can delete your account and all data at any time. Go to Settings, then Reset Account, then Delete Account. You can also email [email protected]. All data is permanently deleted within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Debt Free Day is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy and will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification.
