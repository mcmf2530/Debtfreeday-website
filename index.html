Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

Debt Free Day ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you create an account or use the app:

  • Name and email address (for account creation)
  • Debt information you enter (balances, interest rates, minimum payments)
  • Payment records you log within the app
  • Device information and app usage analytics (anonymized)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve the Debt Free Day app and services
  • Calculate your personalized debt payoff projections
  • Enable partner sharing features when you choose to use them
  • Send account-related notifications (payment reminders, milestone alerts)
  • Respond to your support requests

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure with industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Financial data you enter (debt balances, rates, payments) is used solely to power your in-app calculations and projections. It is never shared with financial institutions or advertisers.

Partner Sharing

If you choose to invite a partner, they will be able to view your debt totals, Debt Free Day date, and payment confirmations. Payment notes and detailed history remain private to you. You can revoke partner access at any time from Settings.

Data Deletion

You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time. To delete your account, go to Settings → Reset Account → Delete Account, or email us at support@debtfreeday.com. All data is permanently deleted within 30 days of your request.

Children's Privacy

Debt Free Day is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or an in-app notification. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@debtfreeday.com.

Terms of Service

By using Debt Free Day, you agree to use the app for lawful purposes only. The debt projections and calculations provided are for informational purposes only and do not constitute financial advice. We make no guarantees about the accuracy of projections, which depend on the information you provide.

You are responsible for the accuracy of the debt information you enter. Debt Free Day is not liable for financial decisions made based on app projections.

We reserve the right to suspend or terminate accounts that violate these terms or are used in a way that harms other users or the service.
